Complete Organic Waste Collection

We take away all kinds of organic waste and ensure it's processed through eco-friendly composting facilities.

These items get taken to specialized composting facilities where green waste is separated from general rubbish and turned into valuable compost and mulch.

Removing green waste promptly prevents trip hazards and eliminates pest attraction on your property.

Eco-Friendly Process

Turning Green Waste into Compost

There's a very good reason why green waste goes to specialized facilities – it gets turned into valuable compost!

When green waste arrives at the facility, it gets mulched and placed into rows or mounds to promote active composting. It's regularly turned and monitored to ensure ideal conditions for creating compost and mulch mixes that are sold back to the public and businesses.

We take care to remove any items that cannot go into composting facilities, ensuring only appropriate organic materials are processed.

Compostable Items

  • Branches and prunings
  • Leaves and lawn clippings
  • Shrubs and plants
  • Tree logs and timber

Not Compostable

  • Flax and bamboo
  • Cabbage tree
  • Kitchen waste
  • Diseased plants
Health & Safety

Why Remove Green Waste Quickly?

Clearing away green waste promptly is important for health, safety, and environmental reasons.

Pest Prevention

Decomposing organic matter attracts rats, mice, and insects. Remove the attraction before it becomes a problem.

  • Prevents rodent attraction
  • Eliminates insect breeding
  • Reduces odors

Safety Hazards

Piles of branches and prunings create trip hazards and can cause damage on windy days.

  • Eliminates trip hazards
  • Prevents wind damage
  • Protects lawn underneath

Environmental Benefits

Professional composting creates valuable soil amendments while preventing harmful gas emissions.

  • Zero landfill impact
  • Creates valuable compost
  • Reduces greenhouse gases
